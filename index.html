01

Storefront & UX

Mobile-first Shopify Plus storefront in Thai and English. Target page load under 3 seconds on mobile.

02

Commerce Engine

Subscription commerce as the strategic core, built on Shopify Plus. Migrated from Magento 1.

03

Identity & CRM

EGG CRM integration for authentication, loyalty, and coupon management. LINE and OTP login.

04

Payments

Omise gateway handling one-time, installment, and recurring subscription billing. PCI DSS compliant.

05

Fulfillment & Operations

Flash plugin (Intrepid) for order routing, inventory sync, and real-time tracking. LON for customer notifications.

06

Marketing, SEO & Analytics

Full tracking suite with GA4, GTM, Meta and TikTok Pixel. SEO-preserving migration from Magento 1 with 301 redirects.

07

Compliance & Infrastructure

PDPA, PCI DSS, RBAC, and data migration. No passwords migrate — all legacy users re-authenticate.

🔒
PDPA
Cookie consent, marketing consent, age gating, deletion rights
💳
PCI DSS
No raw card data in Shopify. Tokenised via Omise. Subscription tokens secured.
🛡️
RBAC
Marketing vs Customer Service role separation. Shopify Plus admin roles.
📦
Data migration
Products, customers, orders, subscriptions from Magento 1. No passwords transferred.
08

Third-Party Integrations

Three core integration pillars. Each is bidirectional — data flows in both directions in real time.

EGG CRM
Loyalty · Identity · Coupons
  • LINE login with CRM verification
  • OTP phone number authentication
  • Real-time loyalty point balance + tier
  • Earn points on every purchase
  • Burn points as discount at checkout (Ph. 2)
  • Single-use and multi-use coupon sync
  • Auto-apply best-benefit coupon in cart
  • Account CRM widget — point history
Omise
Payment gateway · PCI DSS
  • Credit card processing
  • Bank transfer
  • QR PromptPay (Thai market)
  • Local bank installment plans
  • PCI DSS tokenisation for subscriptions
  • Automated recurring billing
  • Retry logic for failed payments
  • Customer notification on failed billing
Flash / Intrepid
Fulfillment · Inventory · Notifications
  • Automated order submission
  • Real-time order status updates
  • Shipment tracking pushed to Shopify
  • Inventory sync — prevent overselling
  • Push notifications via LINE (LON)
  • Post-checkout customer notifications
  • Delivery status to customer account
