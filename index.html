Customer Migration & Login

Two interconnected situations: moving customer data from Magento to Shopify, and handling three login methods with full identity resolution.

Situation 1 — Customer data migration

Three user types live in Magento SQL. Each migrates to Shopify with a tag that tells EGG CRM how to handle the incoming webhook. Type 3 loyalty users require a match-not-duplicate merge.

Situation 2 — Login identity resolution

Every login attempt — email, phone OTP, or LINE — passes through the identity resolver before a session is granted. The resolver queries Shopify customers, Magento migration tags, and EGG CRM in sequence.
